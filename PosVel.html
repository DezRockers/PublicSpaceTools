Position and Velocity from Orbital Elements  
This tool computes the inertial position and velocity of a satellite orbiting the Earth from the  
six orbital elements : semi-major axis (a), eccentricity (e), inclination (i), ascending node (W),  
argument of perigee (w) and true anomaly (u).
